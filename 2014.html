Porsche Macan: 7.5/10

Reliability: 7/10 - This car being manufactured and designed by Porsche has features that help the car be more reliable as it is built with quality and has advanced technology that help as well. Although, the cost of repairs, electronics and driving habits might degrade the reliability.

Safety: 9/10 - This car is very safe as it has various technology that contribute tp the overall saftey this car showcases. These include airbags, ABS, ESC, TCS, blind spot monitoring and many more.

Performance: 8/10 - This car goes from 0-100 in 4.3 seconds (including Sport Chrono Pack) and has a top speed of 253KM/H. This car also has a 2.0L four-cylinder turbo which creates 233BHP as well as AWD with various Porsche suspension options.

Practicality: 7/10 - This car being an AWD and plenty of cargo space as well as high interior comfort is quite practical. Unfortunatley, this car is not very fuel efficient and has high maintenance costs but a part from this, this car is very reliable.

Price: 7/10 - This car can be bought from $30,750 - $45,600 which is good value for a quality Porsche but keep in mind this is a old car so it might have pre-exsisting issues due to its age. So it might be smart to buy a more recent model.

Porsche 918 Spyder LD: 6/10

Reliability: 8/10 - This being a Porsche comes with many features that make this a very trustworthy and reliable car such as its profound build and performance components. Although is important to note that this is a sports car and based on driving habits it will need costly maintanence compared to regular cars.

Safety: 7/10 - This car comes with many components such as TCS, ABS, airbags and many more that help to make it safe but mainly is dependent on the driving habits of the driver. This is because this is a high performance car it drives like one so this should be kept in mind when using.

Performance: 9.5/10 - This car goes from 0-100 in 2.4 seconds and has a collossal top speed of 345KM/H! This car also has a 4.6L naturally aspirate V8 engine that also incorporates electric power and has AWD which adds to the downforce and grip this car holds.

Practicality: 4/10 - This Porsche although performing outstandingly is not very practical for everyday usage. This is due to the ground clearance, limited seating and cargo space and expensive components which degrade the practicality of this car.

Price: 2.5/10 - This is an extremely expensive car with the price starting from $845,000 to $929,000 which would be unwise to spend on. This is because if your going to spend a large amount of money it is wiser to buy a newer model as it will last for longer and most likely have less issues.
