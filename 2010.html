Porsche 911 Type 991: 8/10

Reliability: 8.5/10 - 7 recalls over it's lifetime, small number of reported issues, generally accepted as a very reliable car, needs servicing every two years

Safety: 8/10 - Although with limited information on the web it is a very safe car as it is equipted with the necessary electronics to protect you and manage crash impacts

Performance: 9/10 - This car goes from 0-100KPH in 4.1s and has a top speed of 299.3KPH! It also has a 3.8L flat 6 engine which gives off 385 BHP with RWD and AWD. It also has a sport-tuned suspension with high performance brake discs. Although, this car like many sport cars is not very fuel effiecent with 7-8 KPL.

Practicality: 6.5/10 - Standard editions aren't plentiful with eqipment (although there are lot's of optional extras such as cruise control, sunroof, heated seats etc)

Price: 8.5/10 - Due to the age of this car the price is around $55,000-$100,000 which is good value as this car still is a fast, reliable and safe car.

Porsche Cayenne Type 958 G2: 9/10

Reliability: 6.5/10 - Although this car has great engines on offer as well as build quality it lacks with it's electronics and complexity which are prone to failure which is expenisve to replace.

Safety: 9/10 - This SUV car is a very safe car as it has contrubting features that help this. These include airbags, rollover protection, parking sensors and cameras, lane departure warnings and many more.

Performance: 8/10 - This car goes from 0-100 in 4.9 seconds and has a top speed of 273km/h. It also has a 3.6L V6 engine which produces 296HP and has AWD. Lastly, this SUV has double wishbone front suspesion and multi-link rear suspension.

Practicality: 6.5/10 - This car is quite practical because as an SUV it enjoys 5 seats with an eleven speaker sound system with a CD/DVD player. There is also sat nav with climate control, front and rear parking sensors and many more features.

Price: 9/10 - This cars price starts from as much as $15,300 all the way up to $45,750 due to its age. Although, this does not mean that the car is poor due to the price reflected as it is still a quality car.

Porsche Panamera G1: 8.5/10

Reliability: 9/10 - This car has various saftey, advance electronic features such as traction and stability control that help make it very reliable and trustworthy which it is generally considered to be.

Safety: 8/10 - This is a very safe car to drive as it has a robust shell, multiple airbags, ESC, TCS, brake assist, adaptive cruise control and the list goes on. This makes this sports car a safe car to drive.

Performance: 7.5/10 - This car does 0-100KPH in 4.8 seconds with a top speed of 289KPH. This car also has V6, V8 and hybrid options with AWD.

Practicality: 9.5/10 - This car is a very practical car as it has 4 doors with 4 seats which make it a comfortable and spacey sports car. You can even fold the back seats back and there's room for 44.6 cubic feet of luggage.

Price: 8/10 - This car's price for the entry car starts at $35,000 which is great value considering its over a decade old.
