Porsche Cayman G2: 7.5/10

Reliability: 7/10 - This car being a Porsche is commonly known for it's high build quality and parts which adds to overall relability of this car. Although, due to its high perfomance a wear on components is commonly seen which degrades the relability.

Safety: 8/10 - This car being a high performance Porsche is a very safe car as it includes precise braking, advanced airbags, high-visiblity lights and many more. This car also has a life expectency of 11-13 years with around 150,000-200,000 mileage which will provide a safe ride throughout it's age.

Performance: 9/10 - This car goes from 0-100 in 4.5 seconds with a top speed of 281 KM/H. This car also has a 2.0L turboscharged 4-cylinder boxer engine producing 300HP and is an AWD car.

Practicality: 6/10 - This car although being a sports car has quite comfortable seating but unfortunatley does not have much cargo space, not very efficient, high maintance costs and finally only has 2 seats.

Price: 7/10 - $40,000 - $57,000 which is a pretty good price considering it is a porsche but it is an 10 year old car so it might not be the smarest use of money.
